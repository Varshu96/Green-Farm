- Glasshouses: Glasshouses are the optimal greenhouse structure solution for the commercial production of vegetables, fruits and flowers in mild/cold climates, as well as for nurseries.

- Polyethylene greenhouses: Polyethylene greenhouses and polytunnels are excellent greenhouse structures for nursery cultivation and the commercial production of vegetables and flowers across diverse climactic conditions. They’re particularly good in locations where it is hot and humid outside.

- Nethouse: If you need a greenhouse structure for growing in a moderate climate without diverse swings in temperature then the most profitable and cost-efficient solution might be a nethouse.Nethouses are suitable greenhouse structures for the cultivation of various crops including vegetables, orchards and tree-crop nurseries.Nethouses increase production efficiency and quality – by increasing the security of the crop and reducing water and pesticide use. They’re a cost-effective greenhouse structure for large areas and can deliver great value and returns.
